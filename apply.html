Join the AIDA Lab

We are looking for highly motivated students to join our team. If you are interested in conducting research in artificial intelligence, generative design, machine‑learning based optimisation or intelligent CAD/EDA tools, we encourage you to apply.

Prospective Graduate Students

Candidates for master’s and doctoral programmes should meet the admission requirements of Kyung Hee University’s graduate school. When contacting us, please include the following information:

  • A brief statement of your research interests and how they align with the AIDA Lab.
  • Your CV/resume detailing your educational background, publications (if any) and relevant experience.
  • Academic transcripts.
  • Any examples of prior projects or code repositories that demonstrate your skills.

Prospective Undergraduate Interns

We occasionally host undergraduate researchers during the semester or summer. If you are curious about our work and would like to gain research experience, please send us a short email describing your interests and availability.

Contact Information

For all enquiries and applications, please email us at contact [at] aida‑lab.org. Replace [at] with @ when composing the email. We will respond with further instructions and, if appropriate, schedule an interview.

Our laboratory is located at Kyung Hee University’s Seoul campus. We look forward to hearing from you!
