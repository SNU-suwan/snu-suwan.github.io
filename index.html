AI‑aided Design Automation

Harnessing artificial intelligence to empower the next generation of design tools and methodologies.

About the Lab

The AI‑aided Design Automation (AIDA) Lab is a new research group at Kyung Hee University in Seoul, South Korea. Beginning on 1 September 2025, we focus on the confluence of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automated design processes. Our mission is to build intelligent systems that automate and accelerate the design of complex products – from electronic circuits and mechanical components to architectural structures and beyond. By harnessing data‑driven models and generative algorithms, we aim to reduce design cycles and empower engineers and designers with novel creative tools.

The field of AI‑aided design automation is growing rapidly. Recent research has explored integrating machine learning into electronic design automation (EDA) workflows and using generative models to create design variations that meet specified constraints. For example, work at the University of Rhode Island has highlighted projects such as hardware‑accelerated machine‑learning aided design tools and machine‑learning based design optimization【86325014122364†L51-L76】. These efforts inspire our lab to pursue similar innovations, adapting them to a wide range of design domains.

In addition to algorithm development, we are passionate about bridging theory and application. Our collaborations span academia and industry, aiming to translate state‑of‑the‑art research into practical tools that can be used in real‑world design environments.

Research Highlights

Generative Design

We develop generative models that can automatically propose diverse design alternatives. Generative AI studies patterns in data and learns to create new designs similar to the examples it has seen【685440112580855†L160-L168】, enabling rapid exploration of the design space.

Machine‑learning based Optimization

Our work investigates using machine learning to predict performance metrics and optimize designs. This approach reduces the number of expensive simulations required and enables designers to converge to high‑quality solutions more efficiently【86325014122364†L51-L76】.

AI‑aided Design Tools

We build intelligent CAD/EDA tools that assist engineers throughout the design workflow. By embedding AI modules into existing software, we aim to automate routine tasks and provide data‑driven suggestions, speeding up innovation cycles.
