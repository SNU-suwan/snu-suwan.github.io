About the Lab

The AI‑aided Design Automation (AIDA) Lab is a research group at Kyung Hee University in Seoul, South Korea. We focus on the confluence of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automated design processes. Our mission is to build intelligent systems that automate and accelerate the semiconductor design process. By harnessing intelligent algorithms, we aim to reduce design cycles and empower engineers and designers with novel creative tools.

The field of AI‑aided design automation is growing rapidly. Recent research explores integrating machine learning into electronic design automation (EDA) workflows and using generative models to create design variations that meet specified constraints. These efforts inspire our lab to pursue similar innovations, adapting them to a wide range of design domains.

In addition to algorithm development, we are passionate about bridging theory and application. Our collaborations span academia and industry, aiming to translate state‑of‑the‑art research into practical tools that can be used in real‑world design environments.

Research Highlights

Generative Design

We develop generative models that can automatically propose diverse design alternatives. Generative AI studies patterns in data and learns to create new designs similar to the examples it has seen, enabling rapid exploration of the design space.
